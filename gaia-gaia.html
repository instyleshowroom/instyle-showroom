GAÏA GAÏA

GAÏA GAÏA is a laidback Luxury brand founded in 2020 by Quince & Zoe Karssen. Inspired by art, pop culture and women with a strong sense of independence. The brand aesthetic is casual and feminine but not without an air of masculinity. The garments are both a reflection of freedom and true raw expression. Crafted from superior fabrics and produced in limited quantities, GAÏA GAÏA offers a conscious approach to the future of luxury casual wear.

In the founders’ words: “We are focused on reaching our true customers and therefore limiting over production, we celebrate the Anti Heroes, the individuals that seek something just a little bit different”.
