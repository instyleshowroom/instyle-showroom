Canessa

Canessa was founded in Florence by Andrea Giangrasso and Guia Canessa, with the intention of producing the finest, most durable and timeless cashmere knitwear for men and women who travel the spacetime.
The Canessa family founded Malo Cashmere in 1972.
Passion and know-how are handed down from generation to generation, like a cashmere sweater is passed on from father to son.

Canessa collection is shaped around psychedelic culture, a manifestation of liberty, change, and a strong willingness to live out emotions without fear of exploring the unconscious. in other words: to go beyond...
Produced in the heart of Italy, our cashmere is prepared for a revolutionary wear: avant-garde techniques work it into a fabric, at once compact, soft and flowing.
The knits and garments revisit traditional and artisanal techniques of production, embracing the brand’s modern touches.
