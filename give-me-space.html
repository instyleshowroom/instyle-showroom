Give Me Space

Give Me Space tries to fulfill the millenials needs, by creating a collection of catchy and iconic pieces that play with present topics of interest and that sparkle in its literal meaning. Inspired by space, and noticing its increasing trend in the art, design and fashion world, the creative director, Ginevra Rossini, felt the desire to create a brand that plays with the theme and translates its message: speaking the language of the millenials in a playful manner.
