Ombra

Ombra has been created to provide women and men alike with a capsule of staple items we consider to be foundational for any modern closet.

Gender-fluid and timeless garments, restyled with edgier and modern fits, that could walk with everyone through the different stages of life and stand the test of trends - like a shadow (Ombra) walking with you through the seasons of life.

Ombra is designed by the world-renowned design pioneer Natalie Ratabesi and manufactured in Milan, Italy.
