3x1

3x1 was launched in 2011 by denim designer and entrepreneur Scott Morrison. Scott is a pioneer in the denim industry, with 3x1 he reshaped the way people looked at denim by celebrating the production process at its very finest. In 2021, the brand is back with its authentic and sophisticated denim fits, designed for women who have an eye for detail and craftsmanship.
3x1 is present in more than 200 international points of sale.
