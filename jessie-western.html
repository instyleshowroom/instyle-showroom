Jessie Western

Since the beginning of Jessie Western, created by Jessica and Vicky, the designers’ sole aim is to have jewellery, bags, boots and clothes that are completely handmade and one off. All the Native American Indian artists Jessie Western work with, create from their heart and are not being forced to mass produce. The designers want you to be assured that every item you purchase is genuine, not a reproduction.
‘Keep it real’ is Jessie Western’s philosophy.
